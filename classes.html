Character Classes

Choose to play any of three classes:

WARLOCK

The jedi-like space wizard you've always wanted to be.


Warlocks hold a variety of powers ranging from shooting a laser beam to wielding a flaming sword.
Scroll through the box to see more images.

Subclasses:

  • Dawnblade - Wield a flaming sword and burn your enemies
  • Voidwalker - Practice in the arcane arts of the void
  • Stormcaller - Rain down thunder onto your foes

TITAN

A Spartan-like warrior class that will plow through any foe.


Titans are the do all protect all soldiers that you don't want to mess with.
Scroll through the box to see more images.

Subclasses:

  • Sunbreaker - Wield a flaming hammer that can devastate the very earth
  • Sentinel - Wield a unbreakable shield that you can throw
  • Striker - Plow through any foe and break through any obstacle

Imagine John Wick and Legolas put together...Need I say more?


Hunters are the most agile class and the most evasive class.
